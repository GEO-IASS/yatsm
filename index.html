Yet Another Time Series Model (YATSM)¶

About¶

The YATSM model is based off of tests for structural changes from the econometrics literature including the MOSUM or CUMSUM (Chu et al, Zeileis, and others) as implemented in a remote sensing context by BFAST (Verbesselt, et al. 2012) and CCDC (Zhu and Woodcock, 2014). This effort is not intended as a direct port of either algorithm. The “Yet Another...” within the “YATSM” name intends to refer to these two algorithms without claiming 1 to 1 functionality of either.
